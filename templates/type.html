Commands and their Syntax:

"Create [Document Title]" to create a new document

"Open [Document Title]" to open an existing document

"Insert [word or phrase] after [(different) word or phrase]" to insert text at a specific point

"Delete [word]" to delete a specific word

"Delete from [word or phrase] to [(different) word or phrase]" to delete more than one word

"Copy from [word or phrase] to [(different) word or phrase]" to copy text

"Paste after [word or phrase]" to paste at a specific point

"Replace [word or phrase] with [word or phrase]" to replace certain words or phrases for another

"Replace all [word or phrase] with [word or phrase]" to replace all of the certain words or phrases for another

"Period" to add a period at the end of the document

"Comma" to add a comma at the end of the document

"Period after [word or phrase]" to add a period at a specific point

"Comma after [word or phrase]" to add a comma at a specific point
